MTRCB Rating: NYR

Genre: Animation, Comedy, Mystery

Director: Jared Bush & Byron Howard

Cast: Ginnifer Goodwin (Judy Hopps), Jason Bateman (Nick Wilde), Ke Huy Quan, Quinta Brunson, Fortune Feimster, among others.

Rookie cop Judy Hopps and sly fox Nick Wilde are back, now enrolled in a “Partners in Crisis” program to strengthen their teamwork. But when a venomous snake slithers into Zootopia, they’re plunged into a twist-filled mystery involving new allies—and an unexpected therapist—testing their skills, partnership, and the fabric of their city’s peace.
