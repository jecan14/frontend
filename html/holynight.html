HOLY NIGHT

MTRCB Rating: NYR

Genre: Action, Horror, Supernatural

Director: Lim Dae-hee

Cast: Ma Dong-seok (Ba Woo), Seohyun (Sharon), Lee David (Kim Gun), Kyung Soo-jin (Jung-won), Jung Ji-so (Eun-seo), among others.

When a sinister cult unleashes chaos across Seoul, only “Holy Night”—a trio of demon hunters armed with supernatural abilities—stands between the city and damnation. Led by the indomitable Ba Woo, joined by the exorcist Sharon and tech-savvy Kim Gun, the team fights to save a possessed young girl. But as the final ritual nears and darkness tightens its grip, they must confront their deepest fears to banish evil… or be consumed by it.

Available in: 2D, 3D
